## A better way to learn languages

Most flashcard apps on the market don’t work well for language learning at all.
They are often too expensive, too cumbersome, and they don’t support handwriting
practice or long-term learning.

Our solution, [jadeocr](https://jadeocr.com) (yes, it’s lowercased), is free
and open-source while addressing these concerns for both independent learners
and teachers.

Using jadeocr, teachers can engage students in novel ways outside the classroom
by assigning flashcard decks and quizzes while tracking student progress through
classroom statistics.

Jadeocr received the 3rd place [award](https://eshoo.house.gov/constituent-services/house-student-app-challenge)
in the 2020 Congressional App Challenge in California’s 18th Congressional district.
